关于我

我叫 Ke Fang(有些实名羞耻)，网络昵称是碎瓜或Mazzystar，来自我喜欢的两个乐队。我之前是算法工程师。因为脑子里有太多未完成的想法，从 2022 年底离职并开始个人开发之旅。

我用双语写作：中文是我的母语，让我能自由地使用最精确的措辞完成表达，因为一些文章(#1, #2, #3)登上 Hacker News 首页，有了一批英语读者，所以我也会使用 GPT-4 Claude 将文章翻译成英语，让我的想法触及到世界上更多地区的人们。

我会把一些日常的想法存放在thoughts，把我在网上看到的有趣的东西存放在Zeli 的 Telegram 频道。

My name is Ke Fang, a former machine learning engineer(image/face generation) and now a self-employed developer. Driven by curiosity for new things, I began my solo development journey in December 2022.

I write bilingually: Chinese is my native language, allowing me to express my flair for writing, and English is translated by GPT-4 Claude, enabling me to convey my ideas to a wider audience.

I keep my daily thoughts in thoughts, and interesting things I see on the internet in TG channel.

我的第一个产品是寻隐，它可能是世界上第一个将 CLIP 模型嵌入在手机上从而使用自然语言(“草坪上的黄衣女孩”)搜索相册，我最终将它开源了。另一个是 Dolores，我希望用 LLM+记忆机制，探索 AI 是否能产生"意识"，由于用户的使用转向色情与我的兴趣相违，因此暂停了此项目。我有许多业余"just for fun"项目，如：最佳平替、 AI 海龟汤，它们通常是很短时间内完成的玩具，最佳平替曾在最高有 15 万日活，然后销声匿迹。我对它们没什么期待，只希望能保持好玩、活下去。

My first product is Queryable, which might be the first in the world to embed the CLIP model on mobile phones, enabling natural language search of photo albums (e.g., “girl in yellow dress on lawn”). I eventually open-sourced it. Another product is Dolores, where I hoped to use LLM+memory mechanisms to explore whether AI could generate “consciousness”. However, due to users rapidly shifting towards pornographic content, which contradicted my interests, I’ve temporarily suspended this project. I have many “just for fun” side projects, such as 最佳平替 (Best Alternative) and Turtle Soup. These are usually toys completed in an afternoon or overnight. ‘Best Alternative’ gained 400,000 users within 12 days of launch and reached a peak daily active user count of 150,000. I have no expectations for their future I just hope they remain fun.
